About

Art, language, and structure

My work moves between painting and linguistic research. I explore how voice, aperture, rhythm, and symbolic structure can shape both visual form and meaning.

Through Udha e Zërave and ZË-RO, I am building a world where vowels are not decorative marks, but living centers of relation, tension, memory, and balance.
