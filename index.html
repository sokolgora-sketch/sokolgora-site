About

Painting, language, and structural inquiry

Sokol Gora is an artist and researcher whose work moves between painting, symbolic language, and structural inquiry. Across visual practice and writing, he explores how voice, aperture, rhythm, and relation shape meaning in both image and language.

Two central threads define this work: Udha e Zërave, a mythic and symbolic framework built around the Seven Voices, and ZË-RO, a research instrument for testing vowel-aperture structure and semantic openness. Together, they form a shared investigation into pattern, resonance, and the architecture of meaning.
